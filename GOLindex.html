Welcome to the Game of Life!

Will your cell civilization live or die?


Game of Life Rules:

  1. Any cell civilization with less than two live neighbors dies from underpopulation.
  2. Any cell civilization with two or three live neighbors lives on to the next generation.
  3. Any live cell civilization with more than three live neighbors dies from overpopulation.
  4. Any dead cell civilization with exactly three live neighbors becomes a living cell civilization from reproduction.

Click on any number of cells you would like to start as alive, then click Start to begin the game...
